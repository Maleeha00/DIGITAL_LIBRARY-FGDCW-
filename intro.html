About FGDC(W)

Federal Government Degree College is affiliated with Federal Board of Intermediate & Secondary Education, Islamabad at Intermediate level and at BS level it is affiliated with National University of Pakistan (NUP). This Digital Library is a dedicated web-based platform designed to enhance remote education for students and faculty of our university. It provides seamless access to academic resources, including books, notes, research papers, and other learning materials — all in a digital format. The system replaces the traditional manual library process and aims to make learning more accessible, efficient, and future-ready.
